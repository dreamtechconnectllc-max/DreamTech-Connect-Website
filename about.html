Shawny Hargrove

Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over a decade leading L&D across roles like Director of Curriculum and Training Operations, Shawny founded DreamTech Connect to bring that same enterprise-level expertise to startups that are ready to grow but don't have the time, bandwidth, or internal team to build it themselves. She channels that experience into AI-driven learning systems that make training seamless, scalable, and measurable.

Shawny partners closely with founders and people leaders to build learning ecosystems that align directly to business goals. Whether that's LMS implementation, a certification framework, or a full onboarding program, she designs solutions that don't just check a box. They move the business forward.

If you're ready to transform how your team learns and performs, she's ready to build that strategy with you.

Sean McCullough

Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer

With 19+ years of experience spanning instructional design to Training and Operations Director, Sean co-founded DreamTech Connect to give scaling startups access to the kind of L&D expertise that used to be reserved for large enterprises. He's spent his career inside complex organizations, leading design teams, rebuilding programs that weren't working, and introducing technology that made learning faster, smarter, and more cost-effective.

Sean works closely with every client from day one, starting with the gaps before ever touching a solution. He designs learning programs that are built around your business goals, not a template, ensuring the final product doesn't just train your team. It moves the needle and proves ROI.

If you're ready to stop guessing and start building a learning function that actually performs, he's ready to make that happen.
